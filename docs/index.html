I'm Alex, a PhD candidate (haemodynamics + graph convolutional neural networks) and a trainee magnetic resonance physicist for NHS Wales. Thank you for visiting my little patch of the internet. All views are my own and not that of my employer.

You can expect content on programming, Emacs, philosophy, ethics, magnets and bread. In addition to this, I'll try to post explanations of papers that I publish. If I'm not the first author I'll do my best to explain the paper or, more likely, explain my contribution to paper.

This is site is intentially minimal and left as an exercise to the reader…
