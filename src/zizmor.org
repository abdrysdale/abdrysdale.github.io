After learning about the compromise of [[https://github.com/kubernetes-el/kubernetes-el/issues/383][kubernetes.el]] [fn:1] through GitHub Actions, I decided to rethink my own continuous integration usage.

The security aspects eluded me but thankfully I discovered a tool called [[https://docs.zizmor.sh/][zizmor]] after several people endorsed it on the [[https://old.reddit.com/r/emacs/comments/1rowm5i/first_hacked_emacs_package/][reddit thread discussing the kubernetes.el hack]].

The output is clear and helpfully provides resources to learn more.

Two errors are automatically fixed with:

#+begin_example sh
uvx zizmor . --fix=all
#+end_example

and the remaining version pinning error can be fixed with [[https://github.com/suzuki-shunsuke/pinact][pinact]].

Incidents such as this make me double down on my faith in minimising the number of external dependencies and inspire me to follow in the footsteps of [[https://github.com/LionyxML/emacs-solo/tree/main][Emacs Solo]].
